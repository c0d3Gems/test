What is FunreaktoR about?

Funreaktor is a platform dedicated for the content creators from all over the world. The posted content must be family friendly, otherwise the owner is exposing himself to account termination.

What type of content will FunreaktoR have?

The FunreaktoR platform is all about the fun content such as funny videos, images, gifs. The users will have the posibility to generate income based on their content going viral.

How does it work?

The FunreaktoR creator will post its funny or art material (memes, other funny images, videos, etc). In exchange, the FunreaktoR platform will reward the creator with "plutonium shards", which could then be exchanged for real a currency (usd, eur)

How does FunreaktoR react in case of copyright infringement?

FunreaktoR respects the copyright laws religiously. Therefore, when copyrighted material is posted by someone else but its original creator, the creator is exposing himself to account termination, and a complete freeze of assets generated by the post in question. The post will be removed, and the creator will receive a "strike". A total of 5 strikes will result in irreversible account termination.

How can I report a post?

Each post will have a report reason button associated. The reasons available are: Copyright Infingement, Unrelated content, Violation of FunreaktoR's guidelines (nudity or sexual content, harmful or dangerous content, hateful content, violent or graphic content, harassment, cyberbullying, spam, misleading metadata, scams, threats, personal content, impersonation, etc.)

How does the report process work?

It is imperative that you reach the content creator first, via email, in order to fix the issue, before reporting him for copyright infringement.

If the post author does not comply, the user who reported the post will be asked to complete a form that includes his name, address, telephone number, proof that the copyrighted material belongs rightfully to him/his organization that he represents, in order to prevent false reports.
